Telepharmacy Consulting: Applications & Advancements in Chronic Disease Management

Capstone research (2024) proposing a practical framework for chronic care via telepharmacy—triage, adherence support, MTM automation, and outcomes tracking across state rules.

Telepharmacy Chronic Disease Informatics MTM
Abstract. This paper surveys telepharmacy regulations, synthesizes patient-outcome literature for chronic conditions (e.g., hypertension, diabetes), and outlines a four-part operating model: (1) remote triage/intake, (2) adherence monitoring & just-in-time counseling, (3) MTM task automation & documentation, and (4) data feedback loops for QI. Findings highlight improved adherence and access, with variability driven by state rules and reimbursement mechanisms.
